Assembly Steps

  1. Prepare the backing board: 60 × 60 cm. Recommended: aluminium dibond (3 mm), MDF (6 mm), or acrylic (5 mm).
  2. Print Background_Center_C.png and mount it flush to the backing board.
  3. Print all blade strips from the folders image_A_left_blades and image_B_right_blades. Verify numbers and sides before cutting.
  4. Laser-cut acrylic blades: 5 mm wide × 600 mm tall × 20 mm deep. Recommended: 2–3 mm thick clear acrylic.
  5. Apply Blade_XXX_Left_A.png strip to the left face of each correspondingly numbered blade.
  6. Apply Blade_XXX_Right_B.png strip to the right face of each correspondingly numbered blade.
  7. Attach blades vertically to the backing board, in order (Blade 001 = leftmost). Maintain 5 mm gap between blades.
  8. Allow adhesive to fully cure before mounting.
⚠ Orientation check: The left face (Image A) faces away from the viewer's right. The right face (Image B) faces away from the viewer's left. Verify this before final assembly.
